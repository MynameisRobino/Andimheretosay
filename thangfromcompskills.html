CSS & the UI

When building your one-page app, think of the final purpose of what your doing or sorting with your app before you craft your final User Interface (or "look-and-feel") with CSS.

Is it a blog, or a store product directory, or a image gallery? The main element you are representing will most often dictate how you set up the final page structure.
